Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-14

1. Who this applies to

Faceless Forge Publisher ("the Tool") is a personal automation application run by a single operator for their own social media accounts. This policy covers the data the Tool accesses via connected platform APIs (TikTok, YouTube, and others).

2. What data is accessed

When the operator connects an account (e.g. via TikTok Login Kit), the Tool requests the minimum OAuth scopes needed to publish content on the operator's behalf: basic account identification and video upload/publish permissions. The Tool does not request or access followers' personal data, direct messages, or any data belonging to accounts other than the operator's own.

3. How data is stored

Access and refresh tokens are stored encrypted, locally, on infrastructure controlled solely by the operator. No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third party.

4. Data retention and deletion

The operator may revoke the Tool's access at any time via the connected platform's own account settings (e.g. TikTok's "Manage your connected apps" screen), which immediately invalidates any stored tokens for that platform.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the operator via the contact information on the home page.
